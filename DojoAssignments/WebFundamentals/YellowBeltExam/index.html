Welcome to We Collect Your IT

We offer a free collection service across the UK for the removal of your old IT systems. We safely reuse this equipment by ensureing any sensitive or personal information that was on these machines is either physically removed or destroyed before we reuse them. Using us shall not only benefit the environment by avoiding the waste and disposal of this equipment but shall also help others, as we donate a portion of all the equipment we collect.

Why waste?

The environmental and social benefits of reuse do not only help conserve natural resouraces, but help reduce air and water pollution caused by hazardous disposal. Reuse reduces the amount of greenhouse gas emissions caused by the maunfacturing of new products. It simply makes good sense to let us collecct and reuse your equipment.
